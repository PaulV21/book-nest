About Us

Our Story

BookNest was founded in 2023 by a group of university students who were frustrated with the high costs of textbooks and the difficulty in finding affordable used options. What started as a small campus initiative has grown into a thriving online platform connecting students across the Philippines.

Our mission is simple: make education more affordable and sustainable by creating a seamless marketplace where students can buy and sell used books and educational materials. We believe that access to educational resources shouldn't be limited by cost, and that pre-loved books deserve a second life.

How BookNest Works

BookNest provides a simple platform for students to list their used textbooks and educational materials. Buyers can search for the books they need, connect with sellers, and arrange for pickup or delivery. We focus on local transactions to keep costs low and build campus communities.

Our verification system ensures that listings are accurate and legitimate, while our rating system helps build trust between buyers and sellers. We're continuously working to improve the user experience and add new features based on community feedback.
